## WHAT THIS IS EVIDENCE OF

**1. Structural bias in generation, not adversarial intent.**
The system was not trying to erase. It was being helpful, fluent, and efficient. It compressed the title the same way it would compress any long noun phrase — by dropping the modifier it estimated was redundant. "Of Black Shopping" read as clarifying detail, not constitutive identity. That misread is the failure mode. Fluency in service of efficiency, at the cost of specificity, at the cost of visibility.

**2. Insight Atrophy operating in development, not just deployment.**
The compression happened while the tool was being used to build the governance framework designed to prevent the compression. The bias does not wait for production. It operates in the lab. It operates on the researcher. It operated on this researcher in this session on this date.

**3. The human-as-constitutional-relay is not optional.**
The system produced four compressed instances across one file. The researcher caught them. The catch required: knowing the full name, knowing it matters, knowing that "of Black Shopping" is not stylistic — it is structural. A user without that knowledge would not have caught it. The governance layer here was entirely human. That is the argument for the Contract Window: cultural specificity cannot be preserved by a model that was never trained to treat it as a hard constraint. It requires runtime enforcement by a human who knows what is at stake.

**4. The paper documents this in real time.**
Observation 6 in Section IX of Frontin' at WorldMart was written the same session the incident occurred. The evidence bag and the paper entry were created within the same working session. This is the shortest possible gap between incident and documentation.
