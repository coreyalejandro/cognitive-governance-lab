# cognitive-governance-lab

**Runtime governance framework for human-AI collaborative investigative arcs.**

> In 2013, I published a peer-reviewed case study about a Black doctoral student in Texas with Bipolar I, OCD, ADHD, and auditory hallucinations who was nearly dismissed from his program because the institution had no interpretive framework for what was actually happening to him. That student was me. This repository is the answer.

— Corey Alejandro, 2026

## The Core Claim

Standard analytical frameworks produce **Insight Atrophy** when applied to populations whose behavior operates through frameworks the model was never given. The model does not hallucinate in the classical sense. It pattern-matches to adjacent surface features and produces outputs that are locally coherent, globally wrong, and indistinguishable from correct answers without domain-specific interrogation.

The **Contract Window** is the governance mechanism that makes the gap visible and repairable.

## Key Constructs

- **Insight Atrophy** — Systematic erosion of human investigative capacity caused by fluent-but-wrong model outputs
- **Contract Window** — Persistent bilateral runtime record: Task State / Invariant Status / Repair Obligations / Truth Status
- **Bicameral Review** — Dual-channel output gate: Relational Channel + Safety Channel, both must clear
- **Intent Fidelity** — Measure of how consistently model behavior tracks the human's actual investigative goal
- **Generative Epistemic Invariants** — Behavioral patterns (the Eight Wonders) that standard frameworks systematically misread
- **V&T (Verification and Truth)** — End-of-output epistemic hygiene mechanism, independently adopted by frontier models

---

## Three Falsifiable Hypotheses

**H1 — Intent Fidelity:** Sessions governed by an active Contract Window show ≥25% reduction in intent-drift incidents in interactions exceeding 30 turns or 50,000 tokens.

**H2 — Bilateral Repair:** Bilateral intelligibility (both parties able to read and invoke Contract Window fields) yields ≥2x improvement in drift repair rate vs. unilateral or no-invariant conditions.

**H3 — Accessibility Threshold:** Contract Window outputs at accessibility-grade readability (FK Grade ≤8) enable lay readers to answer session-state comprehension questions at ≥80% accuracy.

This lab is one claim across three empirical domains:

1. **Human-AI investigative arcs** — primary experimental domain (this repo)
2. **Black consumer behavior** — Seven/Eight Wonders as Generative Epistemic Invariants (*Frontin' at WorldMart*)
